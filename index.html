PUBLIC NOTICE ON PREPARATION OF THE FY
2026/27 ANNUAL DEVELOPMENT PLAN (ADP)

The kenya Goverment of Kajiado is in the process of preparing of FY
2026/27 Annual Development Plan (ADP)pursuant to the provisions of
Section 126 of the Public Finance Management Act 2012. The consitution of
Kenya 2010 provides for public participatation in policy formulation whereas
the county Goverment Act 2012 Section 106(4) stipulates
that public participation is mandatory in county planning processes


In light of this the county goverment of Kajiado wishes to invite the general
public private sector organizations Civil society Organization interested
groups youth and all stake holders to attend a consultative meeting to be held
on 20th August at masaai Technical Training institute Kajiado starting
at 10:00a.m


The public are also en couraged to submit written memorable /proposals to
The CECM - The county Treasury County GOVERMENTof Kajiado P.O. Box 11-
01100 Kajiado OR through. the email: dbud@Kajiado.go.ke,budgetkajiado@
gmail.com and copy to cdpokajiado@gmail.com.


Stake holders are advised to align their memoranda/proposals with the
development priorites set out in thr 2023-2027 Kajiado County integrated
Development Plan which can be accessed on the Kajiado county websites at
www.kajiado.go.ke
